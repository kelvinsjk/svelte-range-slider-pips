## Features

![Range Slider Features](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/simeydotme/svelte-range-slider-pips/main/test/public/range-slider.png)

Ability to import to your project as a **pure <Icon name="vscode-icons:file-type-svelte" />&nbsp;Svelte Component**, or as a **Custom Element to your <Icon name="vscode-icons:file-type-vue" />&nbsp;Vue, 
<Icon name="vscode-icons:file-type-reactjs" />&nbsp;React, or <Icon name="vscode-icons:file-type-angular" />&nbsp;Angular project**.

- <Icon name="twemoji:sparkles" /> fully **customisable** (styles/variables)
- <Icon name="twemoji:wheelchair-symbol" /> **accessible** & touch support
- <Icon name="twemoji:thumbs-up-medium-light-skin-tone" /> **multi-handle**
- <Icon name="twemoji:level-slider" /> **range** (min/max)
- <Icon name="twemoji:label" /> floating **labels**
- <Icon name="twemoji:straight-ruler" /> **ruler** notches (pips)
- <Icon name="twemoji:label" /> labels for notches
- <Icon name="twemoji:memo" /> **step function**
- <Icon name="twemoji:wrench" /> **formatter function**
- <Icon name="twemoji:performing-arts" /> **animated**
